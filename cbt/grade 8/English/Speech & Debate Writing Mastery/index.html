Speech Examples
Persuasive Speech Example:

Topic: The Importance of Recycling in Schools

Opening: "What if I told you that with one small change, we could help save our planet?"

Thesis: Implementing school-wide recycling will benefit our environment and educate future generations.

Evidence: Schools that recycle reduce waste by 40% and save thousands annually.

Call to Action: "Start today - use the blue bins, spread the word, be the change!"

Human Reproduction

Sexual reproduction involving male and female reproductive systems

Key Features:

  • Internal fertilization
  • Live birth (viviparous)
  • Long gestation period
  • Parental care of offspring

Plant Reproduction

Both sexual (flowers) and asexual reproduction methods

Key Features:

  • Flowers for sexual reproduction
  • Pollination by wind/animals
  • Seeds and fruits for dispersal
  • Asexual methods (runners, bulbs)

Conduction

Direct transfer through physical contact between materials

Key Features:

  • Needs direct contact
  • Best in solids
  • Molecules vibrate
  • Example: Metal spoon in soup

Convection

Transfer through movement of fluids (liquids or gases)

Key Features:

  • Fluids move heat
  • Creats currents
  • Hot rises, cold sinks
  • Example: Boiling water

Radiation

  • Example: Sun warming Earth

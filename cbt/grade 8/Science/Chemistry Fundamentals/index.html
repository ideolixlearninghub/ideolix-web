Elements

Pure substances made of only one type of atom

Key Characteristics:

  • Cannot be broken down chemically
  • Atoms all have same atomic number
  • 118 known elements
  • Organized in Periodic Table

Compounds

Pure substances made of two or more elements chemically combined

Key Characteristics:

  • Fixed composition by mass
  • Chemical bonds between atoms
  • Properties differ from elements
  • Separated by chemical means

Mixtures

Two or more substances physically mixed

Key Characteristics:

  • Variable composition
  • No chemical bonding
  • Retain original properties
  • Separated by physical means

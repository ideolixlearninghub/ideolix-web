Sentence Types & Punctuation Guide
Simple Sentence
One complete thought with a subject and verb
Compound Sentence
Two simple sentences joined by a conjunction
Complex Sentence
One main clause and one dependent clause
Comma
Used to separate items in a list or clauses
Quotation Marks
Show someone is speaking or to quote text
Apostrophe
Shows possession or forms contractions

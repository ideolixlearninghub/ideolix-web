Chemical Indicators
Litmus Paper
Acid: Red | Base: Blue
Natural dye from lichens. Red litmus turns blue in base, blue litmus turns red in acid.
Phenolphthalein
Acid: Colorless | Base: Pink
Synthetic indicator. Colorless in acidic/neutral solutions, turns pink in basic solutions.
Methyl Orange
Acid: Red | Base: Yellow
pH range 3.1-4.4. Red in strongly acidic, orange at pH ~4, yellow in neutral/basic.
Universal Indicator
Full pH range colors
Mixture of indicators showing different colors across entire pH scale (rainbow colors).

Grade 9 Math: Number Sets

Master integers, rational numbers, and irrational numbers. Learn to classify numbers and understand their properties.

Integers (ℤ)

Whole numbers including negatives, zero, and positives. Closed under addition, subtraction, and multiplication.

Examples: -3, 0, 5, 100

Rational Numbers (ℚ)

Numbers that can be written as fractions a/b where b≠0. Includes terminating and repeating decimals.

Examples: ½, 0.75, -2, 0.3̅

Irrational Numbers

Numbers that cannot be written as fractions. Non-terminating, non-repeating decimals.

Examples: π, √2, e

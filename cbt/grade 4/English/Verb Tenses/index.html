Identify the verb tense in the sentence:

Verb Tenses Guide
Present Tense
Shows action happening now (I walk, she runs)
Past Tense
Shows action that already happened (I walked, she ran)
Future Tense
Shows action that will happen (I will walk, she will run)
Present Continuous
Shows ongoing action now (I am walking, she is running)

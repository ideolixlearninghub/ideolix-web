Elements

Pure substances made of only one type of atom

Characteristics:

  • Cannot be broken down chemically
  • Found on periodic table
  • Examples: Oxygen (O), Gold (Au)
  • Symbol: 1-2 letters

Compounds

Pure substances made of two or more elements chemically combined

Characteristics:

  • Fixed chemical formula
  • Properties differ from elements
  • Examples: H‚ÇÇO, NaCl
  • Can be broken down chemically

Mixtures

Two or more substances physically combined

Characteristics:

  • No fixed composition
  • Can be separated physically
  • Examples: Air, Salt water
  • Keep original properties

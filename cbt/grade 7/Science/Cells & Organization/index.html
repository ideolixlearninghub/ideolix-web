Plant Cells

Specialized cells with unique structures for photosynthesis

Unique Features:

  • Cell Wall (rigid outer layer)
  • Chloroplasts (for photosynthesis)
  • Large central vacuole
  • Regular, box-like shape

Animal Cells

Flexible cells specialized for various functions

Unique Features:

  • No cell wall (flexible membrane)
  • No chloroplasts
  • Small, multiple vacuoles
  • Irregular, varied shapes

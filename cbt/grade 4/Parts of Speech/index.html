Parts of Speech Guide
Noun
A person, place, thing, or idea (e.g., dog, city, love)
Verb
An action or state of being (e.g., run, is, think)
Adjective
Describes a noun (e.g., happy, blue, tall)
Adverb
Describes a verb, adjective, or other adverb (e.g., quickly, very, well)
Conjunction
Connects words or groups of words (e.g., and, but, because)
